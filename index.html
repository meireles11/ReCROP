ReCROP

ABOUT US

ReCROP is a collaborative project focused on transforming Mediterranean agriculture by enhancing sustainability, resilience and productivity. In response to climate change and water scarcity, ReCROP aims to redesign agrosystems through the integration of biotechnology and eco-friendly farming practices. The project promotes biodiversity, soil health, and water conservation by using bioinoculants, climate-resilient crops, and improved cropping systems.

Operating across the Mediterranean region‚Äîincluding Morocco, Egypt, Tunisia, Italy, France, Portugal, and Spain‚ÄîReCROP targets key crops such as vineyards, cereals, and aromatic/medicinal plants. With a strong multi-actor approach, it engages farmers, agricultural associations, and companies to co-develop and test innovative solutions adapted to diverse climatic conditions.

Project Objectives

  • Validate adaptable agroecological farming systems
  • Develop new bioinoculants to boost soil function and crop yields
  • Create omics-based tools for soil biodiversity assessment and crop suitability prediction
  • Analyze socioeconomic factors to support the transition to greener practices
  • Co-design resilient agricultural strategies with local stakeholders

ReCROP offers a new path forward for Mediterranean farming‚Äîscience-based, sustainable, and rooted in the needs of its people and land.
